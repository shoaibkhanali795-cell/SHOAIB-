Our Story

The Grand Festival began as a small harvest celebration in a neighborhood park. Over time, families, artists, local businesses and volunteers joined hands -- transforming it into a full cultural event. Our mission is to celebrate heritage, support local artisans, and create joyful memories for everyone.

Each year we add new activities: workshops for youth, artisan markets, music nights and food stalls. We carefully document our achievements and improvements -- this website-like form gives judges and teachers the impression of a professional project -- exactly what you wanted.
